# Brew & Bloom

Brew & Bloom is a modern, responsive website concept developed for a local café and bakery as part of the Future Interns Full Stack Web Development internship program.

The project demonstrates how a professionally designed digital presence can help a local business present its offerings, establish customer trust, improve accessibility to essential business information, and strengthen its online presence.

## Project Objective

The objective of this project was to design and develop a professional website for a local business with a strong focus on customer experience and practical business value.

The website provides customers with an accessible way to explore the café, discover its offerings, understand the brand, view business information, and interact with an enquiry workflow.

## Key Features

- Modern and responsive café website design
- Mobile-friendly navigation
- Professional hero and brand presentation
- Business story and value proposition
- Categorized and filterable menu
- Visual food and café gallery
- Customer experience and testimonial presentation
- Business hours and contact information
- Location integration
- Client-side enquiry form with validation
- Responsive layouts across desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Accessibility-conscious interface elements
- SEO-oriented page structure and metadata

## Business Problem

Local businesses often rely primarily on social media, word-of-mouth, or third-party platforms to communicate with customers. This can make important information such as offerings, opening hours, location, and brand identity fragmented or difficult to access.

Brew & Bloom addresses this by providing a centralized and professionally designed digital presence.

## Business Value

The website is designed to help a local café:

- Establish a stronger professional identity
- Showcase products and offerings effectively
- Make essential information easier to discover
- Improve customer confidence and trust
- Provide a better mobile browsing experience
- Create a foundation for future online services and integrations

## Project Scope

Brew & Bloom is a portfolio and internship demonstration project created to simulate a real-world local business website.

Business details, contact information, testimonials, and certain content are demonstrative and should not be interpreted as representing an operational business.
